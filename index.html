About Us

The Commission

The Cross River State Independent Electoral Commission (CROSIEC) is the body responsible for organizing, supervising, and conducting elections within the state. Established to ensure that the electoral process is free, fair, and credible, CRSIEC operates independently, guided by the principles of transparency, integrity, and inclusiveness. Our goal is to empower the citizens of Cross River State by providing them with a platform to exercise their democratic rights in a safe, secure, and transparent environment. The Commission is committed to continuous improvement and innovation, striving to set the standard for electoral management in Nigeria.

CROSIEC

What we stand for

Honesty

We are committed to ensuring that all participants in the electoral process—candidates, political parties, and voters—are treated equally, with no bias or favoritism.

Credibility

We take responsibility for our actions and decisions, ensuring that they align with the legal and moral expectations of our stakeholders.

Integrity

We uphold the highest ethical standards in all our operations, ensuring that every election is fair and just.

Transparency

We are committed to conducting all electoral processes openly and clearly, ensuring that every action is visible and understandable to the public.

Our Foundation

What we stand for

Mission

To serve as an Independent, Effective and Honest state Election Management body (EMB) Committted to the conduct of free, fair and credible elections to strenghten Democracy in the state.

Vision

To be the best state Independent Electoral Commission (SIEC) in the country that meets the democratic aspirations of Nigeria.

Intergrity

The Cross River State Independent Electoral Commission is unwavering in its commitment to integrity. We pledge to uphold honesty, transparency, and fairness in every aspect of our work, ensuring that every election is a true reflection of the people's will.

Core-Values

Committed to ensuring credible electoral processes, the commission prioritizes inclusivity and equal opportunity for all political participants. Upholding values of transparency, Fairness and Accountability through dedication and civic engagements

CROSIEC Law

Section 20(1), Volume 3, Cap C18 of the Laws of Cross River State, 2004. This law provides the legal framework for organizing and conducting local government elections in Cross River State. It outlines the powers, duties, and operational procedures of CROSIEC, ensuring that elections are held in accordance with the constitutional provisions and the Electoral Act 2010 (as amended), guaranteeing transparency, fairness, and proper electoral timelines​

New pilot E-voting System

The Cross River State Independent Electoral Commission (CROISEC) is introducing a new electronic voting system, which will be piloted in select local government areas during the upcoming elections. This initiative aims to enhance the efficiency, accuracy, and security of the voting process. The pilot program will allow voters to cast their ballots electronically at designated polling stations, with results transmitted directly to the central database.
